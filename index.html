About the Festival

Art : Tech In Our Orbit is a story-forward celebration of design, code, and play. Wander through a glowing polar world filled with projection caves, AI-drawn ice murals, sensor soundscapes, and lightning micro-talks by artists and engineers.

  • Interactive trail curated for kids and grown-ups alike.
  • Beginner-friendly workshops: creative coding, generative art, physical computing.
  • Night stage with live visuals, algorave sets, and surprise collabs.
